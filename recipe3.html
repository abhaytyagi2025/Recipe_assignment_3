Spicy Miso Ramen

Prep Time: 30 Mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Miso Paste & Chili Oil
  • Soft Boiled Egg
  • Green Onions and Bamboo Shoots

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare the broth by whisking miso paste into hot stock.
  2. Boil the ramen noodles until they are just tender.
  3. Place noodles in a bowl and pour over the spicy broth.
  4. Top with a soft-boiled egg, onions, and extra chili oil.
