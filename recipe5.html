Belgian Waffles

Prep Time: 20 Mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-purpose Flour
  • 2 Eggs (Separated)
  • 1/2 cup Melted Butter
  • Maple Syrup and Berries

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients and whisk in egg yolks and milk.
  2. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites for extra fluffiness.
  3. Pour batter into a preheated waffle iron until golden.
  4. Serve hot with maple syrup and fresh berries.
