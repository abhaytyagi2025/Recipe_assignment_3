Classic Sushi Rolls

Prep Time: 45 Mins

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice (Seasoned with Vinegar)
  • Nori Sheets (Seaweed)
  • Fresh Cucumber and Avocado
  • Pickled Ginger & Wasabi

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo rolling mat.
  2. Spread a thin, even layer of sushi rice over the nori.
  3. Place sliced vegetables in the center of the rice.
  4. Roll tightly using the mat and slice into bite-sized pieces.
