Cheesy Beef Lasagna

Prep Time: 50 Mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Minced Beef in Tomato Sauce
  • Béchamel (White) Sauce
  • Mozzarella & Parmesan Cheese

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Layer meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Add a layer of lasagna sheets followed by white sauce.
  3. Repeat layers until the dish is full, finishing with cheese.
  4. Bake at 180°C until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
