Hyderabadi Biryani

Prep Time: 60 Mins

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • 500g Basmati Rice
  • 1kg Marinated Chicken/Meat
  • Fried Onions (Birista)
  • Saffron Milk & Whole Spices

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Parboil the rice with whole spices until 70% cooked.
  2. Layer the marinated meat at the bottom of a heavy pot.
  3. Add the rice, fried onions, and saffron milk in layers.
  4. Seal the pot and cook on low heat (Dum) for 45 minutes.
